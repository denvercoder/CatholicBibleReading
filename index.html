About this plan

This is a narrative-first Catholic reading plan using Douay-Rheims book names. It includes the Catholic deuterocanonical books and intentionally omits or condenses many census lists, repeated genealogies, and ceremonial sections on this first pass.

One Psalm is suggested each day. The Psalm cycle repeats after Psalm 150.

Your progress, display preferences, and reading streak are stored only in this browser. A streak counts consecutive calendar days on which at least one reading was marked complete.
